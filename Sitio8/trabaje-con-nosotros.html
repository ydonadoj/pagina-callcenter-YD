04Selection Process

Our recruitment process is transparent and designed to find the best mutual fit:

  • Application Review: Our HR team evaluates your resume and cover letter (up to 5 business days).
  • Initial Interview: A phone or video call to discuss your experience and expectations.
  • Skills Assessment: Language proficiency and role-specific skills evaluation.
  • Final Interview: Meeting with the operations or management team.
  • Offer & Onboarding: Job offer, contract signing, and induction program.
