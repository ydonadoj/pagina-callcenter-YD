About Us

Bilingual BPO excellence — Colombia & United States

01Who We Are

Alliance Contact Solutions is a bilingual Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) and contact center company headquartered in Barranquilla, Colombia, with operations serving clients across the United States and Latin America.

We specialize in designing, implementing, and operating customer service, sales, collections, and technical support campaigns for companies that demand excellence, scalability, and cultural alignment. Our teams are trained to the highest US and Colombian service standards, operating 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Our purpose: To be the strategic bridge between our clients' business goals and their customers — delivering every interaction with professionalism, empathy, and measurable results.
